Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System.

Its orbit around the Sun takes 87.97 days

It is the shortest of all the planets in the Solar System.

It is named after the Roman deity Mercury, the messenger of the gods.

Distance from Sun: 57.91 million km

Orbital period: 88 days

Mass: 3.285 × 10^23 kg (0.055 M⊕)
