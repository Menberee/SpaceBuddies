You chose: Neptune


Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun in the Solar System.

In the Solar System, it is the fourth-largest planet by diameter, the third-most-massive planet.

It is the densest giant planet. Neptune is 17 times the mass of Earth, slightly more massive than its near-twin Uranus

Distance from Sun: 4.495 billion km

Orbital period: 165 years

Surface area: 7.618 billion km²

Mass: 1.024 × 10^26 kg (17.15 M⊕)

Radius: 24,622 km

Moons: Triton, Naiad, Thalassa, Nereid, Hippocamp, Proteus, Neso, Despina, Galatea, Laomedeia, Halimede, Psamathe, Larissa, Sao
